Use your space fighter to
destroy the incoming enemy
ships. Avoid their deadly
ion bombs.

Game Controls:
==============

LEFT/RIGHT
Move your ship left/right

PRIMARY FIRE
Fire your active weapon

SECONDARY FIRE
Use extra weapon
(if equipped)


Challenging Stage:
==================

Destroy the incoming enemy
swarms before they can
escape into space.


Swarm Bonus:
============

If you destroy all enemies
of an incoming swarm before
they can take their
positions in the formation,
you score a special bonus.


Perfect Run Bonus:
==================

You score an extra bonus
if you destroy all
incoming swarms before
they start entering their
formation position.
